Privacy Policy & User Content Guidelines

Last updated: December 02, 2025 Need Support?

1) Introduction

We respect your privacy. WishLink is designed to provide a smart and social gifting experience. We do not sell your personal data.

2) Data We Collect (Summary)

  • Account data: email (if provided), username, profile photo
  • User content: wish list items, links, prices (if entered), posts, comments, likes/pins
  • Social connections: friend requests, friend list and interactions
  • Diagnostics & security: crash logs, basic usage/diagnostic data
Tracking

WishLink may show ads using third-party advertising partners (e.g., Google AdMob). If you allow tracking (ATT), ads may be personalized. If you do not allow, you may still see ads.

3) Permissions

  • Photo Library: only when you upload a profile picture or add images.
  • Camera: to take photos inside the app when adding content.
  • Notifications: for friend requests, updates, and account activity.

4) Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): authentication and database/storage services.

5) User Generated Content (UGC) Policy

  • Zero tolerance for objectionable content (hate, harassment, CSAM, pornography, threats/violence, discrimination, illegal content, etc.).
  • Reporting & blocking: users can report and block within the app.
  • Moderation: reported content is reviewed and actioned quickly, typically within 24 hours.
  • We may remove content and restrict/ban accounts violating these rules.

6) Account Deletion

You can delete your account and associated data from in-app settings. You can also request deletion by emailing us.
