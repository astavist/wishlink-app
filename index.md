## Privacy Policy & User Content Guidelines
**Last updated:** December 02, 2025

### Introduction
We respect your privacy. WishLink is designed to provide a smart and social gifting experience. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

### Data Collection & Permissions
To provide our services, the WishLink app may request access to the following:

* **Photo Library:** Used only when you choose to upload a profile picture or add images to your wish list/posts. We do not access your photos without your explicit action.
* **Notifications:** Used to send you friend requests, pin updates, and information regarding your account activity.
* **Camera:** Used for taking photos within the app to add to your list.
* **Firebase Services:** We use Google Firebase for authentication (secure login) and database services to ensure app functionality.

### User Generated Content (UGC) Policy
WishLink creates a community where users can share wishlists and interact. To maintain a safe environment, all users must adhere to the following guidelines:

* **Zero Tolerance:** We have a strictly enforced **zero-tolerance policy** regarding objectionable content. This includes, but is not limited to: defamatory, discriminatory, pornographic, harassing, or hate-speech related content.
* **Reporting & Blocking:** Users have the ability to **block** other users and **report** inappropriate content directly within the app.
* **Moderation:** Reported content and users are reviewed within 24 hours. We reserve the right to remove any content that violates these terms and to eject/ban users who violate these guidelines immediately.

### Account Deletion
If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, you can do so within the app settings or by contacting us at the email above.

### Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy and Guidelines from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
